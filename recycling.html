The Problem

When we spoke with people about their recycling habits, and volunteers at the non-profit "The Center for Hard to Recycle Materials" (CHaRM), we learned that there was a large disconnect between people wanting to recycle and knowing how to do it correctly.

Early Ideation

Early ideas for a product that could reduce recycling error included:

  • An in-home waste and recycling receptacle
  • A standalone device
  • A mobile or web application
