Wet Paint

Role: Artist/Painter

Wet Paint is a simple 'Unity' computer game that uses classic 2D platformer gamplay and handpainted artwork as an exploration of the intersection between traditional and digital creation. It was created in 3 weeks by a team of 4 Georgia Tech students: myself, Avery Bartlett, Mike Adams, and Sammi Hudock.

You play as an alien on a black and white dystopian planet. As you go through the game you find a new 'berry' in each of the levels. Each of these berries is responsible for bringing one color (red, green, and blue) back to your vision.
